How it works?

SevaBhava is a platform which helps you to find as well as create social initiatives in your area. If you are a person looking for volunteering opportunities to help or an individual/organisation looking to get volunteers, raise funds or simpling spread awareness about your initiatives then this is a right place for you!

Want to find what is happening in your locality?

You simply enter your city/district name in the search bar above and you will find all the activities/social initiatives in your area on the map.

Want to Register an initiative?

Once you have signed up, you can create a new social initiative/help with all the details. Once created you can see your help on the map and also, will be provided with sharable links for sharing it with your friends on social media.

Want to join/contribute to an initiative?

Once you find an initiative/help you like or in the area of your interest,you can click on join the initiative button or you can click on it and you will be redirected to the page where the person/organisation had provided all the details related to it. It may include their contact numbers, social media channels and groups.

How will people know about your initiative/help?

Once you register and add all the details then your initiative will be added in our database and will be visible on the map. Every user using this platform will be abe to see it. You will also get links which you can post directly on social media and share it with other people.

Welcome to SevaBhava

We see a lot of amazing people taking up initiatives to help unorganized workers, migrant workers, families living in slums, stray animals through various means and resources. These people often run short of support as not everyone can generate awareness about all the social work they are doing.

This led to a thought on why not create a platform where people can find all the social work happening in their area on a map and connect with them. SevaBhava is a platform just made for that.
