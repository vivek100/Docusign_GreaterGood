Welcome to SevaBhava

We see a lot of amazing people taking up initiatives to help unorganized workers, migrant workers, families living in slums, stray animals through various means and resources. These people often run short of support as not everyone can generate awareness about all the social work they are doing.

This led to a thought on why not create a platform where people can find all the social work happening in their area on a map and connect with them. SevaBhava is a platform just made for that.
